about me

Currently a retail district manager, Benito Gonzalez woke up one day and decided he'd like to learn coding. Well, technically, he had been doing some online shopping and really disliked the functionality of the website. "Wow," he thought quietly to himself "I should be a web developer." and within the week he was enrolled in a coding bootcamp.

Benito is a self-proclaimed Guitar Hero hero due to getting 100% on Misery Business by Paramore literally one time. In his free time he spreads the gospel of Taylor Swift and Little Mix, writes music under the name Bratty Benny, and watches an unhealhy amount of CBS's Survivor.

Benito currently resides in a blue house in Minneapolis, MN with long-term boyfriend, Bobby, and his dog, Leroy Jonathan Brown. He was last seen standing out front of a local record store patiently waiting for the release of Rihanna's highly anticipated 9th studio album.
